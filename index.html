R10 Emergency Response
Toolbox

October 2023

This R10 Emergency Response Toolbox web app contains QuickStart Guides, air monitoring information, sampling information, and other information that may be referenced in the field in various emergency response scenarios.

Offline Access

This web app is available offline. However, certain operating systems and devices may clear unused cached files after a set period of time, and the file set may be updated periodically. Therefore, it is best practice to open this page (the homepage) once prior to visiting a potential offline area.

Mobile Access

To access the web app from a mobile device, simply navigate to this URL on your browser app (Chrome and Safari are recommended for Android and iOS, respectively)

It is recommended that you add this web app to your home screen. This will provide better access to the app, and will improve the offline capability.

->On iOS Safari, while viewing the home page of the web app, tap the Share icon (square with arrow pointing out of it) at the bottom of the screen. Then, scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"

->On Android Chrome, while viewing the home page of the web app, tap the button with the three dots at the top right of the screen. Then tap "Add to Home screen"
